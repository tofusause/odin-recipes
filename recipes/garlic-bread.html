garlic bread end product

Garlic bread is a popular side dish made from slices of bread—typically baguette or Italian loaf—topped with a mixture of butter, minced garlic, and herbs like parsley. It's often baked or toasted until the edges are golden and crisp, offering a warm, flavorful bite with a rich, garlicky aroma.

Ingrediants

  • bread
  • butter
  • garlic
  • parsley
  • salt

Instructions

  1. preheat oven to 375
  2. slice the bread
  3. mix softened butter with minced garlic, chopped parsley, and pinch of salt
  4. spread the mixture
  5. place bread in oven for 10 minutes
  6. serve and enjoy
